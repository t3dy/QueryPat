RESEARCH DESIGN REQUIREMENT

The studies must preserve evidentiary layers.

Never collapse them early.

For PKD research this distinction is critical.

Use a four-lane evidence architecture.

Lane A
Primary fiction
(stories, novels, essays)

Lane B
Exegesis and self-theorizing
(Exegesis notebooks, letters, interviews)

Lane C
Biographical and critical scholarship

Lane D
Synthesis layer
(dictionary entries and topic dossiers)

Every analytical claim must be traceable to A, B, or C.

The synthesis layer must explicitly distinguish them.
